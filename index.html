Long LaMP

Long-text generation is seemingly ubiquitous in real-world applications of large language models such as generating an email or writing a review. Despite the fundamental importance and prevalence of long-text generation in many practical applications, existing work on personalized generation has focused on the generation of very short-text.

LongLaMP provides a comprehensive and diverse evaluation framework for personalized long-text generation.
